  • Content Processing Engine: Extracts structured data from PDFs
  • Speech Interface: Real-time voice interaction using STT and TTS
  • NLU Engine: Processes questions and identifies knowledge gaps
  • Response Generator: Creates personalized explanations
  • Knowledge Graph: Maps educational concepts and relationships
  • Learning Analytics: Tracks progress and adapts teaching
